## About The Project


**Wild Eyes** is a cool new educational observation game, combining the excitement of the children’s game **“I-Spy”** and the thrill of accelerated learning.  


Players are challenged to identify an object selected by **AI**, and once they find it, they’ll uncover **interesting facts and real-world facts** for it. The game emphasizes **objects found in nature**, helping users discover and appreciate the world around them in a fun and interactive way.


---


### Key Features
-  AI selects objects for players to identify  
-  Learn fascinating facts about nature and science  
-  Encourages observation, memory, and curiosity  
-  Simple, engaging, and educational gameplay experience  
-  Leaderboeard to keep track of top users

## How to play


1. Focus the camera and click to take a **picture**.
2. The AI will select an object for you to identify, and gives you a clue to guess the object .
3. Type or speak your answer.
4. Once you guess the answer, the AI will award you points;
    - If you get the answer wrong **3 times**, the AI will reveal the answer automatically, and you will not receive any points.
5. Once the answer has been revealed, the AI will give you a fact about the object.
6. **Rinse and Repeat!**
